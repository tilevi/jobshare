About

What is JobShare?

JobShare is a site that allows users to create and share DarkRP jobs for the game Garry’s Mod.

Garry's Mod

Garry’s Mod, or simply GMod, is a sandbox physics game. One of the most popular game modes for GMod is DarkRP.

DarkRP

DarkRP is a role-playing game mode that is rooted in "the idea of a simulation of urban life."
In this game mode, players take on roles in the form of jobs. For example, a player may choose to be a Gun Dealer and sell weapons.

Problems

DarkRP server owners are always looking for new jobs to keep the role-playing experience on their servers immersive.
However, many server owners tend to tirelessly search the web and look for DarkRP jobs (and their code).

Moreover, players are always suggesting new jobs to keep their favorite servers from going stale.
If players want to suggest jobs, they often have to post suggestions on community forums (and many times without the code).

Solution

JobShare helps solve these problems by making it easy to create and share DarkRP jobs with others.

For example, server owners can either view a job directly (given a URL) or search among all of the public jobs. The resource URLs and code can then be used as tools to create a new job. Server owners may also choose to create and store DarkRP jobs.

Furthermore, players can create or find suggested DarkRP jobs and then share them with server communities.
